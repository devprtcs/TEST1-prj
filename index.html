    Front End Design

    Design Verification

    We have the one of the strongest team in DV. Our team can execute verification from scratch of complex SoC’s and IP’s by using latest methodologies such as SV-UVM, UPF and meeting key KPI such as 100% functional and code coverage.

    Front End Design

    ASIC Design

    Developing high quality RTL is challenging because the chip needs to be low on area and power consumption and at the same time provide adequate performance. To overcome this, we leverage dozens of years’ experience of our team and follow stringent design checklist.

    Front End Design

    FPGA based Design

    Our engineers have expertise in all the leading FPGA devices such as from Xilinx, Altera, Microsemi and are up to date with latest methodology/tools to produce efficient results.

    Back End Design

    Physical Design

    We provide support throughout RTL to GDSII stages of ASIC development flow. Our experienced team has developed advanced flows for power aware synthesis (UPF, CPF), timing constraint generation (STA), netlist floor planning for best possible PPA and place and route(PNR) for overcoming ever increasing complexity.

    Back End Design

    Custom Layout

    Our expert custom layout team has worked on lower nodes and latest FinFet/FDSOI technologies.

    Back End Design

    DFT

    With rising mission critical application and competition, inserting testing capability in the design stage of the chip is ever so important.

    Analog Mixed Signal Design

    RF/Analog

    Our expertise is mainstream in the field of RF, High Speed Analog & Classic analog. Both in Design and Layout. Our engineering team has worked on 7nm to 500nm, BCD to Finfet etc.

    Analog Mixed Signal Design

    Mixed Signal

    Mixed Signal is ever challenging due to shrinking spaces. We develop Std cells, Memories and IOs as part of Mixed signal. We touch 7nm to 28nm for Memory and Std Cells & much beyond for IOs.

    Analog Mixed Signal Design

    Custom Layout & Misc.

    We have worked on variety of projects requiring custom support. Be it Extraction & other quality enhancement or Support in custom flow & automation. We have supported most of the foundry’s technology and tools.
